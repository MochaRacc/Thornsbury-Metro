Major disruptions to Avandale, Moorinallea & Strathmore commuters

Commuters for the Avandale, Moorinallea and Strathmore lines will experience major disruptions while we work on the Crossrail tunnel project, this includes level crossing removals, station renewals and various other works.

Works will commence throughout Mid-March right up to around October, check the Service Changes page to see how you might be affected.
